Hi fellow art lover! I created this website so that I could view the most popular manga-like art on Twitter. All tweets are sorted by their number of likes and only the most recent tweets are displayed.

You will find mostly mangastyle art, but the algorithm I use also accepts other digital art styles.

I hope artists can find this website useful and art enjoyers can discover new talented artists to follow.

Please join the discord if you love art or have any feedback about the website!
